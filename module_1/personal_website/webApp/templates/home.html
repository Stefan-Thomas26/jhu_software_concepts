Biography

Hello!

My name is Stefan Thomas.

I'm a signal processing engineer at Raytheon that supports the development and refinement of various algorithms for different large-scale systems that utilize electro-optical sensors. My work focuses on developing, testing, and analyzing algorithms that support sensor modeling, performance evaluation, and system-level validation. I'm always looking for opportunities to tackle challenging and exciting technical problems for both simulation environments and embedded systems.

I was born and raised in New York, but I moved to Tucson, Arizona a little over 3 years ago for my current job, my first one out of college. I graduated from the City College of New York with a B.E in Biomedical Engineering.
